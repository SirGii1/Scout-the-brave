🐾 The Legend of Scout the Brave

In the summer of 2025, the world met an unlikely hero: Scout, a fearless 5-pound Pomeranian from Vancouver, Canada. While the average investor runs from "bears" 🐻, Scout showed us what true courage looks like.

When a real bear stormed into his home and ate his food, Scout didn't panic. He didn't sell. He didn't retreat. He charged headfirst at the bear, barking it back into the woods and defending his territory with unstoppable Small Dog Energy. 🐕⚡️

The footage went viral, turning Scout into a global symbol of bravery. The internet crowned him the "Tiny Titan," the underdog who literally chased the bear away.

Now, his spirit lives on in Scout the Brave ($SCOUT) — a meme token that embodies the same fearless energy.
